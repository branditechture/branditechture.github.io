About Our Blog

Blog by Branditechture is an independent publishing platform developed by Branditechture Design Agency to provide unmatched analysis and insights into utilizing technology to power up your business or startup.

Our Dreams are fearfully huge and our aims high, but with the skyrocketing costs of VPS hosting, we had to fall back to the use of static web hosting – All thanks to Github Pages.

Our commenting system is powered by Facebook Comments, since server-side preprocessing is currently not supported on Github Pages.

Blog by Branditechture aims to be a haven for disruptive business owners and entrepreneurs who are looking to supercharge their business using technology.

We write original articles on Tech, Startups, Employee Life, Digital Marketing, Business Hacks, Graphic Design, Branding, Business Automation, Integrations and Management, CRM, and Web Design and Optimization.
